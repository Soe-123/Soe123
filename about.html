Sam Ocean Eccles (SOE) is a graphic designer living and working in London. Upon graduating from Kingston School of Art, he has looked to work with clients who are operating within design, art, or the wider cultural sector. His main two focuses are print and websites, for which he is a trained front-end developer. With all his projects he aims to use a concise typographic approach to celebrate the collaborators work rather then distract from it.
